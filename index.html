Hi, I'm Arnold.

I’m a fourth year at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor studying Life Science Informatics and Applied Statistics.

I’m also a software programmer mainly experienced in designing and developing software for not only the web but also Windows. From intuitive single-paged websites to complex, module-based data analysis tools, I strive to not only create beautiful applications but also beautiful code: concise, intuitive, and clean.

I love the process of bringing an application to life and the idea that no matter how perfect it may be, there is always work to be done in refactoring, updating, and expanding the scope of the project. The fast-paced and constantly growing field of technology excites me as I’m always looking for opportunities to learn and share this new knowledge. In the end, my goal is to create something exciting and beautiful.

On the chance that I’m not typing away, I’m probably deeply involved in photography, practicing my music prowess on the piano, or rock climbing.
